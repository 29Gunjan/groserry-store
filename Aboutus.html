About Us

Welcome to My Grocery Store, owned by Gunjan, your trusted source for all your grocery needs. We are dedicated to providing you with high-quality products and exceptional customer service.

Established on March 12, 2024, My Grocery Store is located at Bazar Chowk, Saoner-441107. Our store opens daily from 9 AM to 9 PM, ensuring that you have access to fresh produce and daily essentials when you need them.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality grocery products at affordable prices while delivering excellent customer service and a convenient shopping experience.

Our Vision

To be the leading grocery store that everyone trusts for quality and value in their shopping experience.
